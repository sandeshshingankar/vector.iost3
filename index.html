FAQ

Got Questions?
We've Got Answers

What services does Vector.ioST offer? +

We offer a full range of professional digital services including custom website development, mobile app development, digital marketing, SEO, e-commerce solutions, UI/UX design, AI-powered tools, and both online and offline support services. Every service is tailored to your business goals.

Are all services paid? Is anything free? +

Yes, all our services are paid. We invest real expertise, time, and resources into every project we take on, and our pricing reflects the professional quality we deliver. We do offer a free initial consultation so we can understand your needs before presenting a tailored quote.

How do I get started with a project? +

Simply reach out via our contact form or buy a service package that fits your needs. Our team will get in touch to schedule a discovery call, understand your requirements, and provide a detailed proposal with timeline and pricing. We move fast once a project is confirmed.

How long does it take to deliver a project? +

Timelines depend on the scope of the project. A standard website typically takes 1–3 weeks. Mobile apps and complex platforms may take 4–12 weeks. We always provide a clear timeline upfront and keep you updated at every stage of delivery.

Is my data and project information kept secure? +

Absolutely. We treat every client's project with strict confidentiality. We're happy to sign NDAs before any work begins, and all project files, credentials, and data are handled securely and never shared with third parties.

Do you provide post-delivery support? +

Yes. All our projects include a support period after delivery to handle any bugs or adjustments. For ongoing maintenance, updates, and growth support, we offer dedicated maintenance packages so your digital products stay fast, secure, and up to date.
