FAQ

Got Questions?
We've Got Answers

Do I need to know how to code? +

Not at all! BuildHub is designed from the ground up to be accessible to everyone. Our drag-and-drop builder and AI Studio let you create professional products without writing a single line of code. But if you're a developer, you'll love our code export and API access features too.

Can I migrate my existing website? +

Yes! Our migration wizard supports imports from WordPress, Squarespace, Webflow, Wix, and more. We also offer a free concierge migration service on Professional and Enterprise plans, where our team does it for you.

What's included in the 14-day trial? +

Full access to every feature on your chosen plan — no restrictions, no watermarks, no catch. You don't even need a credit card. If you decide to continue after 14 days, just add billing. If not, your project data is exported and yours to keep.

How does the AI content generation work? +

Our AI Studio is powered by the latest large language models. Describe what you want — a landing page, blog post, product description, email sequence — and it generates high-quality output in seconds. You can train it on your brand voice for even better results.

Is my data secure and private? +

Absolutely. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we never sell or share your data. Enterprise plans include private cloud deployment options for maximum control.

Can I use a custom domain? +

Yes, all plans include custom domain support with automatic SSL certificates via Let's Encrypt. Enterprise plans can also use wildcard subdomains and dedicated IP addresses for advanced setups.
